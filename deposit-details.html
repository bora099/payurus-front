Learn how to deposit funds to your Payurus account step by step.

How to Make a Deposit

account_balance

Step 1: Log in to Your Bank App

Start by opening your mobile banking app or accessing your online banking portal. Make sure you're logged in with your credentials.

💡 Tip:

If you're using mobile banking, ensure you have a stable internet connection. For online banking, use a secure browser and avoid public Wi-Fi networks.

send_money

Step 2: Navigate to Money Transfer

Once logged in, find and open the money transfer section. This is usually labeled as "Transfer", "EFT", "Wire Transfer", or "Send Money" depending on your bank.

📍 Where to find it:

  • Mobile apps: Usually in the main menu or home screen
  • Online banking: Look for "Transfers" or "Payments" tab
content_paste

Step 3: Enter Recipient Information

You'll need to enter the bank account details provided by Payurus. These details are unique to your account and will be shown in your Payurus app or dashboard.

Required Information:

person Recipient Name: Payurus Financial Services Inc.
credit_card IBAN: Your unique IBAN (found in your Payurus account)
account_balance Bank Name: Global Bankası A.Ş.
attach_money

Step 4: Enter Deposit Amount

Enter the exact amount you wish to deposit. Make sure to double-check the amount before proceeding, as this cannot be changed after the transfer is initiated.

⚠️ Important:

  • Enter the amount exactly as shown in your Payurus deposit request
  • Some banks may charge transfer fees - these are separate from your deposit amount
  • Minimum deposit amounts may apply - check your account for details
description

Step 5: Leave Description Blank

Most banks have an optional description or reference field. For Payurus deposits, you must leave this field completely blank. Do not enter any text, numbers, or special characters.

Why this matters:

Any text in the description field may cause delays in processing your deposit or result in the transfer being rejected. The system automatically matches your deposit using other information.

check_circle

Step 6: Review and Confirm

Before finalizing the transfer, carefully review all the information you've entered. Verify the recipient name, IBAN, amount, and ensure the description field is blank.

After Transfer:

Once you confirm the transfer, the funds will typically appear in your Payurus account within 1-3 business days, depending on your bank's processing time.

You'll receive a notification in your Payurus app once the deposit is processed and credited to your account.

warning

Important Information

• You must make the transfer only from your individual bank account registered in your name. Corporate accounts or third-party transfers are not accepted.

• The account holder name on your bank account must match the name registered in your Payurus account.

• Always use the IBAN provided in your Payurus account. Using incorrect account details may result in failed transfers or delays.

• Keep your transfer receipt or confirmation number for your records. You may need it if you contact support.

• If your deposit doesn't appear in your account after 3 business days, please contact our support team with your transfer details.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my deposit to appear?

Most deposits are processed and appear in your Payurus account within 1-3 business days. Processing times may vary depending on your bank and the time of day you initiate the transfer.

Can I deposit from someone else's bank account?

No, you can only deposit funds from a bank account that is registered in your own name. This is required for security and compliance purposes.

What should I do if my deposit hasn't arrived?

If your deposit hasn't appeared after 3 business days, please contact our support team. Have your transfer confirmation number and bank statement ready to help us locate your deposit.

Is there a minimum deposit amount?

Minimum deposit amounts may vary. Please check your Payurus account or contact support to learn about the current minimum deposit requirements for your account type.

Are there any fees for deposits?

Payurus does not charge fees for deposits. However, your bank may charge transfer fees. These fees are separate from your deposit amount and will be deducted by your bank.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the deposit process or have questions, our support team is here to help you.
