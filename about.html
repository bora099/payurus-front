About Payurus

At Payurus, we believe that finance should be accessible, empowering, and inclusive. We combine the worlds of traditional payments with real, practical, and relevant solutions—helping individuals and businesses take control of their financial lives.

Our Vision

To accelerate global financial access through the widespread adoption of digital payment solutions.

Our Mission

To make digital finance accessible, secure, and efficient for everyone.

Ana Temellerimiz

Güven, hız ve güvenliği temellerimiz olarak dijital ödemelerin geleceğini inşa ediyoruz.

Hızlı ve Kolay Ödemeler

Payurus, dijital çözümlerin istikrarı ve esnekliği ile sorunsuz, küresel ödemeleri mümkün kılar. Ödeme çözümlerimizle neredeyse anında ödeme yapabilir, gönderebilir ve alabilirsiniz. Günlük işlemleri kolay ve çoklu pazar ödemelerini herkes için erişilebilir, güvenli ve verimli hale getiriyoruz.

Uyumluluk ve Güvenlik

Güven ve güvenlik yaptığımız her şeyin merkezindedir. Payurus, sıkı uyumluluk standartlarını karşılar ve fonlarınızın ve verilerinizin her adımda korunduğundan emin olur.

Fast & Easy Payments

Payurus enables seamless, global payments with the stability and flexibility of digital solutions. With our payment solutions, you can make, send, and receive payments almost instantly. We make daily transactions easy and multi-market payments accessible, secure, and efficient for everyone.

Smart Spending with Payment Cards

We provide payment card access to users worldwide; seamless card payments through global networks. As a leading payment fintech, we offer unique access and reliability. We make spending easy, secure, and truly cross-border.

Global Payment Solutions

Our specialized payment infrastructure allows users to securely send and receive money worldwide through secure systems. We make fast and reliable payments accessible to more markets than ever before.

Global Payment Access at Your Fingertips

Payurus combines traditional finance with digital assets, enabling everyone to access and use modern payment solutions. Our multi-currency accounts and payment systems offer direct access to digital payments—expanding global access for everyone.

Compliance & Security

Trust and security are at the heart of everything we do. Payurus meets strict compliance standards and ensures that your funds and data are protected at every step.

Licensed & Regulated

We operate as a fully authorized payment service provider, adhering to strict regulatory frameworks. This strong regulatory foundation allows us to provide secure and compliant payment services on a global scale.

Enterprise-Level Security

Assets are securely held in segregated wallets by our licensed custodial partners. Private keys are securely stored in high-level HSMs meeting FIPS 140-2 Level 3 standards.

Strong KYC and AML Compliance Protocols

We partner with leading providers to offer comprehensive Know Your Customer (KYC), Anti-Money Laundering (AML), and risk monitoring processes; these are compliant with global standards and continuously updated according to the latest requirements.

Multi-Layer Protection

We are PCI DSS Compliant, ensuring your data meets the highest security standards. End-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, transaction screening, secure storage, and regular third-party audits keep your assets and data safe.
