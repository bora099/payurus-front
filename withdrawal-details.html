Withdrawal Details

Learn how to withdraw funds from your Payurus account step by step.

How to Make a Withdrawal

account_balance

Step 1: Access Your Payurus Account

Log in to your Payurus account through the mobile app or web portal. Make sure you have sufficient balance in your account to cover the withdrawal amount and any applicable fees.

💡 Tip:

Before initiating a withdrawal, verify that your account balance is sufficient. You can check your current balance on the main dashboard.

account_balance_wallet

Step 2: Select Your Bank Account

Navigate to the withdrawal section and choose the bank account where you want to receive the funds. If you haven't added a bank account yet, you'll need to add one first.

Adding a Bank Account:

info The bank account must be registered in your name
security You'll need to provide your IBAN and bank details
verified The account will be verified before you can use it for withdrawals
attach_money

Step 3: Enter Withdrawal Amount

Enter the amount you wish to withdraw. Make sure the amount doesn't exceed your available balance and accounts for any processing fees that may apply.

⚠️ Important:

  • Check for minimum and maximum withdrawal limits
  • Processing fees may apply - these will be shown before confirmation
  • Ensure you have enough balance to cover both the withdrawal and fees
preview

Step 4: Review Withdrawal Details

Carefully review all the withdrawal information displayed on the screen. Verify the bank account details, withdrawal amount, processing fee, and the total amount that will be deducted from your account.

Double-check:

  • Bank account number and IBAN are correct
  • Withdrawal amount matches what you intended
  • Total deduction amount (withdrawal + fees) is acceptable
check_circle

Step 5: Confirm and Submit

Once you've verified all the details, confirm the withdrawal request. You may be asked to authenticate the transaction using 2FA or other security measures.

After Submission:

Your withdrawal request will be processed within 1-3 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email and notification once the funds are transferred to your bank account.

You can track the status of your withdrawal in the transaction history section of your Payurus account.

warning

Important Information

• Withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days. Processing times may vary depending on your bank and the time of submission.

• Please ensure your bank account information is correct and up-to-date. Incorrect details may result in failed transfers or delays.

• The bank account must be registered in your name and match the name on your Payurus account.

• Processing fees may apply to withdrawals. These fees will be clearly displayed before you confirm the transaction.

• If your withdrawal hasn't arrived after 3 business days, please contact our support team with your transaction reference number.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my withdrawal to be processed?

Most withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days. The exact processing time depends on your bank and the time you submit the request. Weekend and holiday submissions may take longer.

Are there any fees for withdrawals?

Processing fees may apply to withdrawals depending on your account type and withdrawal amount. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm the transaction, so you'll know exactly how much will be deducted.

What should I do if my withdrawal hasn't arrived?

If your withdrawal hasn't appeared in your bank account after 3 business days, please contact our support team. Have your transaction reference number and withdrawal details ready to help us locate and resolve the issue.

Is there a minimum or maximum withdrawal amount?

Yes, there are minimum and maximum withdrawal limits. These limits may vary based on your account type and verification level. Check your account settings or contact support to learn about the specific limits for your account.

Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

Withdrawal requests can usually be cancelled if they haven't been processed yet. Check your transaction history to see if a cancellation option is available. If the withdrawal is already in process, you'll need to contact support for assistance.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the withdrawal process or have questions, our support team is here to help you.
