Transfer Details

Learn how to send money to anyone using Payurus step by step.

How to Send Money

person

Step 1: Access Transfer Section

Log in to your Payurus account and navigate to the "Send Money" or "Transfer" section. This can be found in the main menu or on your dashboard.

💡 Tip:

Make sure you have sufficient balance in your Payurus account before initiating a transfer. You can check your balance on the main dashboard.

contact_mail

Step 2: Enter Recipient Details

Enter the recipient's phone number or email address. The recipient must have a Payurus account. If they don't have one, they'll receive an invitation to create an account.

Recipient Options:

phone Phone Number: Enter the recipient's registered phone number
email Email Address: Enter the recipient's registered email address
verified The system will verify if the recipient has a Payurus account
attach_money

Step 3: Enter Transfer Amount

Enter the amount you want to transfer. Make sure the amount doesn't exceed your available balance. Transfers between Payurus users are typically free.

⚠️ Important:

  • Check your available balance before entering the amount
  • Transfers between Payurus users are instant and free
  • Some transfers may have minimum or maximum limits
note

Step 4: Add Optional Note (Optional)

You can add an optional note or message to accompany your transfer. This helps the recipient understand the purpose of the transfer.

💡 Tip:

Adding a note can be helpful for personal transfers, bill payments, or business transactions. Keep it brief and clear.

check_circle

Step 5: Review and Confirm

Before confirming, carefully review all the transfer details: recipient information, amount, and any optional note. Once confirmed, the transfer will be processed instantly.

After Transfer:

The transfer is processed instantly. The recipient will receive a notification immediately, and the funds will appear in their Payurus account right away.

You'll receive a confirmation notification, and the transaction will appear in your transaction history.

info

Transfer Information

• Transfers between Payurus users are instant and free. No processing fees apply.

• The recipient will receive a notification immediately when you send money.

• Both you and the recipient must have active Payurus accounts for the transfer to be processed.

• You can send money using either the recipient's phone number or email address.

• All transfers are secure and encrypted. Your financial information is protected at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a transfer take?

Transfers between Payurus users are processed instantly. The recipient will receive the funds immediately and get a notification right away.

Are there any fees for transfers?

Transfers between Payurus users are completely free. No processing fees or charges apply.

What if the recipient doesn't have a Payurus account?

If the recipient doesn't have a Payurus account, they'll receive an invitation to create one. Once they sign up and verify their account, the transfer will be completed automatically.

Can I cancel a transfer after sending it?

Since transfers are processed instantly, they cannot be cancelled once sent. However, you can contact the recipient and ask them to send the money back if needed.

Is there a limit on how much I can transfer?

Transfer limits may apply based on your account type and verification level. Check your account settings or contact support to learn about the specific limits for your account.
