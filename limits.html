Important Information

• Limits may vary based on your account verification level and account type.

• Higher limits may be available after completing additional verification steps.

• Limits reset at the beginning of each day and month based on your account timezone.

• For questions about your specific limits, please contact our support team.

• Fees are calculated at the time of transaction and may vary by payment method.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I increase my transaction limits?

You can increase your limits by completing additional verification steps in your account settings. Higher verification levels typically offer increased transaction limits.

When do limits reset?

Daily limits reset at midnight in your account's timezone. Monthly limits reset on the first day of each month.

Are limits the same for all account types?

No, limits vary based on your account type and verification level. Business accounts typically have higher limits than personal accounts.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

If you attempt to exceed your limit, the transaction will be declined. You'll need to wait until the limit resets or contact support to request a temporary limit increase.

Are there any fees for transactions?

Deposits are free. Withdrawals have a 1% fee (minimum $5). Member-to-member transfers have a 0.5% fee. Card payments have a 2.5% fee.
